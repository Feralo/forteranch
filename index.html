About Forte Ranch

Forte Ranch is on the property that Once belonged to an Italian Rancher named John Forte who owned 19 acres of land between Turnpike Road and San Antonio Ave. The land was used to farm lemons and avocados. In the early 1980s, a developer named Hugh Thompson purchased the property and soon received County approval to build 80 condominiums (phase 1) with the possibility of 80 more in a 2nd phase.

Hugh envisioned building the homes in a fashion that would inspire a neighborhood atmosphere. Consequently, Bob Croisdale was selected as the exclusive agent to perform all sales and marketing functions at Forte Ranch. The sales and construction began in June of 1995. The middle of the 1990’s was a low point in Santa Barbara real estate market, and beginning such a large project was risky, Fortunately, the risk has paid off.

As part of the original team, Bob became intricately involved with all aspects of Forte Ranch development. He was involved in the planning & design decisions, C,C & R decisions, marketing and sales. He has been a part of the Forte Ranch neighborhood for over 20 years and he is the best person to speak with about all aspects of your Forte Ranch: history, marketing analysis, sales data and more.
